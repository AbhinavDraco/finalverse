Privacy & Policy

DracoVerse is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of DracoBlog, DracoDaily, and DracoCommunity.

Data we collect
Information gathered when you use DracoVerse

When you create an account or interact with any DracoVerse service, we may collect the following types of information:

👤 Username 💬 Messages & comments 📝 Blog posts 👍 Votes & reactions 🖼️ Uploaded images 💻 Browser & device type 🕐 Usage timestamps 📧 Email (letter only)
We do not collect passwords in plain text. Admin authentication is handled locally and never stored on our servers. Guest usernames are session-scoped and stored only in your browser's local storage.

We only collect what is strictly necessary to provide you with a functioning community experience. You are never required to provide real personal details — a pseudonymous username is sufficient.

⚙️
How we use your data
Purposes for which your information is processed

Your data is used solely to provide, improve, and personalise DracoVerse services. Specific uses include:

🐉 Community features
Displaying your username, avatar, and profile across DracoBlog, DracoDaily, and DracoCommunity so other members can engage with your content.
📊 Analytics
Aggregated, anonymised data to understand which content resonates most, fix bugs, and improve performance over time.
📬 letter
If you subscribe, we use your email to send periodic updates about new content and community highlights. You can unsubscribe anytime.
🛡️ Safety & moderation
Admin tools use message history and usernames to enforce community guidelines, apply timeouts, and remove harmful content.
We never sell your personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
🔗
Data sharing
Third-party services we rely on

DracoVerse uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate. Your data may be processed by these providers under their respective privacy policies:

🔥 Firebase / Firestore (Google)
Stores blog posts, messages, votes, comments, and poll data in real time. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
📈 Google Analytics
Anonymised page-view and engagement metrics. No personally identifiable information is shared.
📢 Google AdSense
May display contextual ads and collect cookie data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.
🖼️ Microlink API
Fetches open-graph preview images for article links. Only the URL of the article is shared.

We do not share your data with any other parties. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

🕐
Data retention
How long we keep your information

Content you post — blog posts, comments, community messages — is stored in Firestore until you or an admin deletes it. Username and profile preferences saved in your browser's local storage persist until you clear your browser data.

letter email addresses are retained only while you remain subscribed. You may unsubscribe at any time and your address will be removed promptly.

⚠️ Deleted messages and blogs are removed from our Firestore database immediately and cannot be recovered. Please be certain before deleting content.

Uploaded images are stored as base64 data within Firestore documents. Deleting the associated message or blog post also removes the image data.

✅
Your rights
Control over your personal information

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to honouring these regardless of legal obligation.

👁️Access the data we hold about you and request a summary of what information is stored.
✏️Correct inaccurate or outdated information by editing your profile or contacting us.
🗑️Request deletion of your account, messages, or blog posts at any time.
📤Export your content (posts, comments) in a portable format upon request.
🚫Object to certain data processing activities, including analytics tracking.
🔕Opt out of the letter at any time using the unsubscribe option in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We aim to respond within 7 days.

🍪
Cookies & local storage
How we use browser storage

DracoVerse primarily uses browser local storage rather than traditional cookies to remember your session. This data never leaves your device unless you post content to the community.

💾 Local storage (our site)
Stores your username, avatar, gradient preference, notification settings, bookmarks, and channel unread counts.
🍪 Third-party cookies
Google Analytics and Google AdSense may set cookies in your browser. These are subject to Google's own privacy policy.

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings. To opt out of Google Analytics, visit tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. To manage ad personalisation, visit adssettings.google.com.

✅ We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes on our own platform.
🔒
Security
How we protect your information

All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, which provides industry-standard encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS). We apply input sanitisation across all user-generated content to prevent cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks.

Admin features are protected by a server-verified password. No user can impersonate the admin role or access restricted moderation tools without the correct credentials.

While we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data, no online service can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use a unique username and avoid sharing sensitive personal information in public messages or blog posts.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users as promptly as possible.

Policy changes
How we handle updates to this document

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Significant changes will be announced in the #announcements channel of DracoCommunity. Continued use of DracoVerse after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

⚠️ We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
