🐉 Our Story

About DracoVerse

Why this project exists, and where it's headed

DracoVerse began as a simple question: why should someone need five different tabs open just to keep up with technology? One tab for the latest AI tools, another for a place to write, another to talk to like-minded people. DracoVerse was built to bring all five of those tabs into a single, cohesive place.

The Daily Poll exists for a simple reason: reading is often a one-way experience. Polls flip that around for thirty seconds a day, letting the community weigh in on a question and instantly see how their answer compares to everyone else's.

DracoVerse is maintained by a single independent developer, Abhinav, rather than a large team or company. That means updates happen directly based on real feedback, and the roadmap is shaped by what the community actually asks for — more categories, better search, deeper community tools, and beyond.

If you have feedback, a feature request, or just want to say hello, the Community hub and the Privacy & Policy page both list ways to get in touch.
